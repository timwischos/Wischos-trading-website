## KEY INSIGHTS

### Price Tier Analysis
- **Under $2/pc (low-cost items):** WP-104 (multi-tool pen), WP-203 (letter opener), WP-205 (bookmark), WP-303 (money clip), WP-306 (nail clipper), WP-307 (scissors)
- **$2-5/pc (mid-range):** WP-101 (bolt pen), WP-102 (dual pen), WP-201 (mouse pad), WP-202 (pen holder), WP-204 (propeller opener), WP-206 (spinning top), WP-301 (RFID wallet), WP-302 (key organizer), WP-403 (tumbler), WP-404 (bamboo mug)
- **$5-10/pc (premium):** WP-103 (tactical pen), WP-207 (fidget stick), WP-304 (Ti comb), WP-305 (pry bar), WP-402 (Ti flask), WP-405 (Ti cup)
- **$10+/pc (high-value):** WP-401 (Ti vacuum bottle), WP-103 premium, WP-402 premium

### Material Cost Hierarchy (per unit, approximate)
1. **Zinc alloy (die-cast):** Cheapest — $0.10-0.30 raw material per small item
2. **Stainless steel 304:** Low-mid — $0.30-1.00 per small item
3. **Aluminum (6061/anodized):** Mid — $0.50-1.50 per item
4. **Brass (C360):** Mid-high — $1.00-3.00 per item
5. **Carbon fiber:** High — $2.00-5.00 per item
6. **Titanium (TC4/Gr5):** Highest — $3.00-15.00+ per item

### MOQ Patterns
- **Most flexible (MOQ 50-100):** Tactical pens, EDC tools, titanium items
- **Standard (MOQ 200-500):** Mouse pads, desk accessories, wallets, key organizers
- **Higher MOQ (500-1000+):** Budget pens, nail clippers, scissors, bookmarks
- **Titanium drinkware:** MOQ 500-5000 (capital intensive)

### Margin Opportunities (Retail vs. Wholesale)
| Product | Wholesale | Western Retail | Markup |
|---------|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Brass bolt-action pen | ~$3.50 | $30-80 | 8-23x |
| Brass spinning top | ~$3.50 | $30-50 | 8-14x |
| Ti vacuum bottle | ~$15 | $60-120 | 4-8x |
| CF fidget stick | ~$4.50 | $15-25 | 3-6x |
| RFID wallet | ~$2.50 | $15-30 | 6-12x |
| Ti EDC comb | ~$7 | $25-45 | 3-6x |
| Multi-tool pen | ~$1.50 | $8-15 | 5-10x |

### Manufacturing Hubs
- **Yongkang, Zhejiang:** Titanium/SS drinkware ("China's Hardware Capital")
- **Shenzhen, Guangdong:** EDC tools, fidget toys, electronics
- **Yiwu, Zhejiang:** Promotional gifts, pens, small metal items
- **Zhongshan, Guangdong:** Fidget spinners, metal crafts
- **Yangjiang, Guangdong:** Scissors, nail clippers, cutting tools

### Price Trend (2024-2025)
- Titanium products saw **significant price drops**: small-cap Ti vacuum bottles dropped from ~$11 to ~$5.60 per unit
- High-end titanium: $45-70 fell to $15-40 wholesale
- General metal promotional items remain stable due to mature supply chains

### Payment & Logistics (Standard)
- **Payment:** T/T 30% deposit + 70% before shipping; also Western Union, PayPal
- **Delivery terms:** EXW, FOB, CIF, DDU available
- **Sample time:** 7-15 days
- **Production time:** 15-45 days (varies by complexity)
- **Shipping:** Express (DHL/UPS/FedEx) 5-10 days; Sea freight 25-35 days
