## 10. EVA Foam Custom Inserts (Die-cut, per piece)

| MOQ | Price/Unit (USD) | Notes |
|-----|-----------------|-------|
| 100 | $0.80 – $2.50 | High per-piece due to die amortization |
| 300 | $0.50 – $1.50 | |
| 500 | $0.30 – $1.00 | |
| 1000 | $0.15 – $0.60 | |

**Factors affecting price:**
- Number of cutouts: 1–2 cutouts (single item) vs 3–5 cutouts (multi-item set)
- Foam thickness: 10mm–40mm (thicker = more expensive)
- Foam density: 25D (soft) to 45D (firm) — higher density +10–20%
- Flocking/velvet surface: +$0.20–$0.50/unit
- Custom color: standard black/white free; custom color +$0.05–$0.15/unit at MOQ 500+

**Setup/Tooling:**
- Die-cut mold: $30–$100 (simple) to $100–$250 (complex multi-cutout)
- CNC routing (no mold needed): $0 tooling but +$0.10–$0.30/unit production cost
- Die cutting is much cheaper than injection molding for EVA

## Key Findings & Recommendations for Wischos

### 1. MOQ 100 is challenging but doable
Most rigid box factories prefer MOQ 500+. At MOQ 100, expect:
- 30–50% price premium over MOQ 500 pricing
- Limited customization (1–2 color printing, basic foil stamp)
- Longer lead times (factories prioritize larger orders)
- **Strategy:** Find packaging suppliers who specialize in small-batch/sample orders, or use semi-custom approach (stock box + custom sleeve/sticker)

### 2. Best value packaging strategy for MOQ 100
- **Option 1 — Semi-custom:** Stock black magnetic box + custom belly band + branded sticker seal. Cost: ~$4.00/set
- **Option 2 — Fully custom:** Custom magnetic box with 1-color foil logo + EVA insert. Cost: ~$5.50/set
- **Option 3 — Hybrid:** Branded individual product boxes + unbranded outer gift box. Allows reuse of product boxes across different set configurations.

### 3. Tin boxes are NOT practical at MOQ 100
Custom-printed tin requires MOQ 3,000–10,000. Workaround: plain stock tin + branded sticker.

### 4. Wooden boxes are premium but expensive
At MOQ 100, wooden boxes cost $8–$18/unit — only justified for products with high enough margin. Reserve for VIP/showcase orders.

### 5. Biggest cost savings at scale
Moving from MOQ 100 → MOQ 500 typically saves 30–50% on packaging per unit. This is the single biggest lever for reducing packaging costs.

### 6. Packaging as % of unit price
For Wischos gift sets (estimated $15–$40 wholesale unit cost), packaging represents:
- Economy: ~5–12% of unit cost
- Standard: ~15–25% of unit cost
- Premium: ~25–40% of unit cost

Industry benchmark for B2B promotional gifts: packaging should be 10–20% of wholesale price.
